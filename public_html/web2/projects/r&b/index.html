R&B (Rhythm and Blues) and Soul are music genres with roots in African American culture, blending jazz, blues, and gospel influences. R&B started in the 1940s and is known for its catchy rhythms and smooth melodies, often about love and life. It evolved over time, incorporating funk, disco, and modern sounds. Soul is a more emotional style of R&B, with powerful vocals and heartfelt lyrics. It focuses on deep feelings, often about love and struggle.
