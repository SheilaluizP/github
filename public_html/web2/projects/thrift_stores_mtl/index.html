About

Welcome to ThriftGuide, your ultimate guide to discovering the best thrift stores near you and beyond! Whether you're a vintage hunter or just love finding unique finds on a budget, ThriftGuide connects you to a curated collection of thrift, vintage, and consignment shops from The Plateau of Mount Royal. Browse stores by personal interest, style, or depending on your personality. Each store profile features photos, store hours and specialties (like furniture, designer brands, or retro fashion). Click on each store to visit their website!

Marché Floh

Located in 4301 Saint Denis St, Montreal, Quebec H2J 2K9

Marché Floh is a vintage clothing company that curates and sells fashion from the past and present. This store seeks to offer small businesses and entrepreneurs opportunities to launch their unique projects in a low risk retail environment.

A fun fact about Marché Floh Market:

Every item sold at Marché Floh has been hand selected by a team of skilled vintage curators, with the purpose of providing quality and diversity to their clients.

60s gogo dresses and ‘90s streetwear to early-2000s throwbacks, leather bags, and band tees.

Carole Thrift Shop

Located in 4321 Saint Denis St, Montreal, Quebec H2J 2K9

Carole Thrift Shop is a clothing store that promote sustainable fashion by offering second-hand clothing to Canadian citizens and reducing the environmental impact of fast fashion. They believe that giving clothing a second life reduces the demand for mass-produced, low-cost clothing, which often relies on unsustainable practices and labor conditions.

A fun fact about Carole Thrift Shop : All items found in store are curated vintage clothing
