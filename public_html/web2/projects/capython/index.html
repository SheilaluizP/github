Description

For this project, my team of 7 members decided to create an ad compaign for a game called "A Sprout Game". Sprout Game is an exciting journey where you explore a world of growth and challenges. It's a mix of strategy, adventure, and creativity! We chose this project to highlight our topic: Focusing on sustainability and innovation; As well as our keyword: Amplify.

My role

My role was to design the layout of the poster, the banner and the game cover.

Challenges

  • Figuring out the layout: I had to design a layout that's reasonable and easy to understand our message. For example, placing the bulb as the "center piece" of the whole layout along with the title of the game.
  • Proportions of each elements: The main element that I displayed on all my work is the environment of the game. It contained the bulb and many infrastructures. It was a challenge for me to figure out the right proportions of each element.
  • Communication with the whole team: We were 7 members in my team and we all had different schedules, meaning that we had to mainly use a social platform to communicate with each other but not necessarily mean that everyone will check the group chat.

    • Solutions

  • Getting feedback from my team in person: This solutions solved many confusion and hesitation throughout the whole week because it's given in a direct and a clear understanding on the issue rather than on text.
  • Asking teacher's for help: This was a game changer since I didn't have a lot of experience on any of the Adobe softwares. So asking help from a teacher can save a lot of time.

    • What I learned

    I learned that working in a team requires a lot of patience and that we need to be open-minded. Regarding my role, I had to wait until the halfway point of the week to fully start placing everything to their destinated area. Since, I prepared the potential layout for each poster. As for being open-minded is that not all ideas may be used in the final product ,and that it's best to keep in mind what's best for the team.
