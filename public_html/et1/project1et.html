Project 1: 3d Design

Description

My final 3D project is more of a feminine necessity. I made a claw clip holder along with a jewelry compartment. As for including a function element, this product has a 360° rotation feature to browse through each claw clip. The reason why I chose to create this organiser is simply because I own too many claw clips but somehow manage to always lose them every time. So, I’ve decided that this was a great opportunity to make something useful to stop this never ending lost and never found hair clips. As for the jewelry compartment, I just added it since it would be more practical and leaves more free space on my nightstand. I even made a divider to separate my rings and chains since they tend to get tangled. As for the color of the PLA filament, I kept simple and neutral, the color white. This product is composed by 3 different parts : the base, the claw clips holder and the jewlery organiser.
