LIA project: Bubble Tea Advertisement

Description

As for my last project in Emerging Tech 1, I would like to use Blender. I’ve decided to create a bubble tea advertisement. There isn’t really a specific reason why I chose this product, other than the fact that it’s in fact my favorite beverage. It’s a calming drink that comes with any kind of flavor your heart desires along with distinguish toppings for texture. With these combinations, they will forever leave an interesting taste to your palette. In result for you wanting more. I’ll use Blender as the main tool for this project to 3D animate the overall advertisement, along with the background, the colors, etc. In the 3D animation, I’ll be inserting many bubble tea flavors and many toppings that my classmates prefer in their drink. My goal regarding the duration of this short video, I’m aiming for at least 20 seconds.
